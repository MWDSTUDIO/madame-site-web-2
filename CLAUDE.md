# CLAUDE.md — Madame Wedding Design (permanent project rules)
## Before creating or editing ANY page or article
- Heading structure is LAW: follow 06-STRUCTURE-H1-H2-H3-SEO.md templates for every
  new page/article (wedding/commission page, destination hub, venue article, FAQ).
- ONE H1 per page; strict H1→H2→H3 order, no skips; never use headings for styling;
  main keyword in the H1 and <title>; titles end with " | Madame Wedding Design";
  metas 140–160 chars containing a verifiable fact; every page opens with 1–2
  sentences of direct answer under the H1.
- Published weddings: photographer's name in the meta + schema.
- Schema.org on every page (Organization, Article, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList);
  alt text always pulled from data/media.json.
## Brand & voice rules (no exceptions, ever)
- Visible name: "Madame Wedding Design", never "MADAME" alone.

  visible copy to Paris.

  Destination & Logistics. NEVER "partial planning" or "month-of".
- Budgets: nothing under $100,000; threshold stated only in FAQ + Investment step.
- Version C voice: concrete, editorial, Easton/Marcy Blum register; banned
  vocabulary: dream wedding, magical, fairy tale. Portfolio sections use wedding
  language ("Our latest weddings", "Wedding highlights" — Estelle, 20/07/2026);
  "commission" may season prose sparingly, never section titles.
- Entry threshold: hunter green + ivory ONLY — no gold, champagne, or metallics.
- Clean URLs, no .html; lowercase-hyphen keyword-rich slugs.
- Never overwrite corrections already made manually by Estelle.
