A standard of the house

Privacy & discretion, by design

Discretion is not a policy we adopted; it is how the house works. Non-disclosure agreements by default, closed venues, vetted teams, and the least data we can hold — stated plainly below.

For our families

Every member of our team, and every artisan and vendor we commission, signs a non-disclosure agreement before learning a family's name. Venues are contracted for exclusive use and closed to the public for the duration of the production. Guest information travels on need-to-know documents and is destroyed after the celebration. Photography and film belong to the family; the press sees a commission only when the family decides it should — and on their terms, reviewed by us and by their advisors. Where a family requires it, we work alongside their security detail and family office as one team.

For our visitors

This site holds the least data it can. The enquiry form sends us exactly what you type — nothing more — and we use it only to reply to you. We set no advertising trackers and sell nothing to anyone. If you ask to receive our journal, that consent is separate, explicit, and revocable by one email to hello@madamewedding.design.

Your rights (GDPR)

You may ask at any time what personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct it, or ask us to erase it. Write to hello@madamewedding.design and we will answer within one month, as the General Data Protection Regulation requires. Data is processed in the European Union; our registered contact address is available on request.
