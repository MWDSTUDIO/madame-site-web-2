A letter from the house

Great celebrations are not decorated — they are produced. Behind three perfect days in Tuscany stand eighteen months of work: the estate secured before it ever reaches the market, the artisans commissioned by name, the arrivals of two hundred guests from four continents choreographed to the minute, the weather plans nobody will ever know existed.

This is our work. We accept a small number of weddings each year, and we hold every thread of them — planning, design, and production — so that our families are guests at their own celebration. Curated & produced with intention. For those who know.

Estelle BogaertFounder & Creative Director

What the house does

Capabilities

Full Planning & Production

The complete commission: strategy, budget architecture, estate and venue negotiation, artisan direction, guest logistics across borders, and flawless on-the-ground production — one house, accountable for everything.

Creative Direction

The aesthetic language of the celebration — concept, design, tablescapes, florals, lighting, paper, wardrobe counsel — composed as one coherent world and executed with couture discipline.

Destination & Logistics

Multi-day celebrations across the world: charters and transfers, accommodation of entire estates, security protocols, and the discreet choreography large gatherings demand.

Privacy & Discretion

Non-disclosure agreements as standard, closed venues, vetted teams, and press handled on our families' terms — published only when, and how, they wish.
