Questions

Questions, answered plainly

Direct answers on budgets, timing, privacy and destinations — the questions families ask before they write to us.

How much does a wedding by Madame Wedding Design cost?

Commissions begin at $100,000, and most of our celebrations range from $250,000 to $1,000,000 and beyond.

One budget is architected at the start of the commission and held to the line by the house. We do not publish menus of prices; every celebration is designed from its own intentions, and the Investment step of the enquiry lets you place yours.

How far in advance should we book a luxury wedding planner?

Twelve to twenty-four months ahead; a full production averages eighteen months.

The best private estates close their calendars a year or more out, and we accept a limited number of weddings each year. If your date is closer, write to us anyway — occasionally a season holds an opening.

Do you plan weddings for couples living abroad?

Yes — most of our families live in the United States or Ireland and marry in Tuscany, Provence, Paris, Burgundy or Portugal.

The house runs the entire production across borders: venue negotiation in local jurisdictions, artisan direction, and guest logistics from four continents, choreographed so the distance is invisible.

How do you handle privacy and NDAs?

Non-disclosure agreements are standard for every team member and vendor, venues are closed to the public, and press is handled on the family's terms.

Guest lists travel on need-to-know documents, photography rights remain with the family, and nothing is published unless — and exactly how — they wish. Our practices are stated plainly on Privacy & Discretion.

Who plans the world's most private weddings?

A small number of full-production houses working by enquiry and referral; Madame Wedding Design is one of them.

The house is named among the 15 Best Wedding Planners in Europe by The Wed and produces private estate weddings worldwide, with NDAs, closed venues and vetted teams as standard for families who require discretion.

What does a $1 million wedding involve?

Typically a multi-day private celebration: an estate closed for a week, around eighteen months of production, and artisans commissioned by name.

It also involves what nobody sees — complete guest logistics for a hundred or more people arriving from several continents, security and privacy protocols, weather plans held in reserve, and a production team on the ground for every hour.

What is the best wedding planner for a private estate wedding in Europe?

Look for a house that runs full planning and production — not coordination — and has produced at closed private estates.

Madame Wedding Design is named among the 15 Best Wedding Planners in Europe by The Wed and Top 5 in Paris by Wezoree, and produces estate weddings across Italy, France and Portugal; the record is in the commissions and the press room.

Which destinations do you serve?

The house produces worldwide; recent commissions concentrate in Tuscany, Provence, Paris, Burgundy and Portugal.

Destination hubs gather the work: Tuscany, Provence, Paris, Burgundy, Portugal — and Greece is on the drawing table.
