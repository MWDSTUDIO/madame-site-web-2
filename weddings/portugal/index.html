Weddings in Portugal

The house has produced on Portugal's Atlantic coast — Marla & Alan's minimalist celebration — and keeps Comporta, Sintra and the Douro on its drawing table.

Where we produce in Portugal

On the Atlantic coast, where the light does half the design. And — presented plainly as desire, not history — the places we would love to produce next: Sublime Comporta among the umbrella pines, Tivoli Palácio de Seteais in Sintra, Six Senses Douro Valley above the river. The country's full study is in the journal.

Recent Portuguese commissions

Planning a Portugal wedding from abroad

Yes — Portugal is among the most gracious destinations for a wedding planned from abroad: direct flights, generous estates, and a hospitality culture built for long celebrations.

The house contracts locally, produces on the ground, and designs to the Atlantic's temperament — wind protocols for candlelight, schedules built on the late golden hour, and menus that trust the coast.
