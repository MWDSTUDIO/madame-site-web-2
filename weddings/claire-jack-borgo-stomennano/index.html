Borgo Stomennano, Chianti

A wedding weekend for Claire & Jack of Philadelphia in a private Italian borgo — crystal chandeliers, crimson blooms, and a village given over to one family. Published by Style Me Pretty in 2026.

A Borgo Stomennano wedding gives one family a private Chianti village for a whole weekend. For Claire & Jack of Philadelphia, Madame Wedding Design produced a multi-day Tuscany destination wedding — published by Style Me Pretty in June 2026.

The wedding

Claire and Jack came from Philadelphia with a precise appetite: a whole village to themselves, several days rather than one, and a palette with conviction — crimson against old stone. Borgo Stomennano, a private hamlet in the Chianti hills, gave them all three.

The production

The house took the borgo for the week: every house on the square held a guest, every meal moved to a different corner of the hamlet, and crystal chandeliers were rigged over the courtyard by a team that had them down again before Sunday lunch. Style Me Pretty published the weekend in June 2026.
