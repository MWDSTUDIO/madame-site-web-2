Burgundy — Over The Moon

A château in Burgundy

Aimee Connolly & John Greene came from Dublin to a Burgundian château for a weekend the house produced end to end — featured in Over The Moon.

The commission

An Irish couple, a French château, and two hundred reasons the two usually don't mix: distance, language, logistics. Aimee and John wanted Burgundy without the friction — their people, moved across a sea and seated under plane trees as if they'd always summered there.

The production

The house contracted the château for exclusive use, ran charters from Dublin, and staged the weekend across the park, the orangery and the cellars — with an Irish band flown in for the last night, because some things do not translate and should not have to. Over The Moon told the story.
