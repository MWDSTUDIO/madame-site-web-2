Weddings in Paris

The house produces Paris weddings in palaces and private mansions — Shangri-La Paris, Hôtel d'Évreux on Place Vendôme, the Ritz — including celebrations published in Vogue and Brides.

Where we produce in Paris

At Shangri-La Paris, the former Bonaparte residence facing the Eiffel Tower; at Hôtel d'Évreux, the last private mansion receiving on Place Vendôme, with Potel & Chabot at the stoves; at the Ritz Paris; and at Château de Villette, forty minutes from the city. Paris venues are small, listed and demanding — exactly why they reward full production.

Planning a Paris wedding from abroad

Yes — a Paris wedding is the easiest of our destinations to reach and the most exacting to produce: the house handles monuments' rules, curfews and formalities so the evening simply happens.

Civil formalities in France take preparation for non-residents; many of our couples marry legally at home or at the mairie ahead of the celebration. The house counsels on the sequence, then produces the evening itself to the minute.
