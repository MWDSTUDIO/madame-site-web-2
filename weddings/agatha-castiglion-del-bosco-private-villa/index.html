Tuscany — thirteen guests

A private villa, Castiglion del Bosco

Agatha's wedding seated thirteen people. It received the same production the house gives three hundred — because intimacy is a scale, not a shortcut.

The commission

Thirteen guests, one villa on the Castiglion del Bosco estate, and a couple who wanted the smallest possible room with the highest possible standard. Small weddings expose everything: at thirteen, there is no crowd for a detail to hide in.

The production

A chef's table built for the villa's loggia, a string trio in the olive grove, and a timeline with the same contingency depth as our largest productions. The estate itself — voted best resort in Europe by Travel + Leisure readers — appears in the journal.
