Weddings in Burgundy

The house produces château weddings across Burgundy — celebrations featured by Martha Stewart Weddings, Carats & Cake and Over The Moon — for families from Ireland and the United States.

Where we produce in Burgundy

At private châteaux across the Côte-d'Or and beyond — contracted for exclusive use, with the park, the orangery and the cellars staged across a full weekend. Burgundy is the connoisseur's France: quieter than Provence, greener than the south, and generous to multi-day formats built around its tables and its wines.

Recent Burgundian commissions

Planning a Burgundy wedding from abroad

Yes — Burgundy works remarkably well from abroad: one house on the ground, charters into Paris or Lyon, and a weekend the guests never have to navigate themselves.

Two of the commissions on this page crossed a sea to happen — Aimee & John from Dublin, Cindy & Franklyn with a Paris civil ceremony first. The pattern holds: secure the château early, let the region set the menu, and give the last night to the family's own music.
