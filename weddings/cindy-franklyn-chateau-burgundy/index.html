Burgundy & Paris

A civil ceremony in Paris, then a château in Burgundy dressed against type — Cindy & Franklyn's celebration was featured by Martha Stewart Weddings and Carats & Cake.

The commission

Cindy and Franklyn married twice in one week: first before the officiant in Paris, then before everyone they love in Burgundy. The design turned a stone château hall tropical — a deliberate collision the couple carried completely.

The production

Two cities, one production office: the Paris formalities handled to the letter, the château contracted for the weekend, and arrangements flown in and conditioned on site. Martha Stewart Weddings and Carats & Cake both told the story.
