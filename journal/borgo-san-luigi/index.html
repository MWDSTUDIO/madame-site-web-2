Borgo San Luigi: a Tuscan borgo for a full wedding weekend

Borgo San Luigi gathers stone farmhouses, gardens and pools into one Tuscan hamlet near Monteriggioni — a venue that houses every guest inside the celebration, which is the whole secret of an effortless weekend.

Why Borgo San Luigi

Because logistics decide how a wedding feels. When a hundred guests sleep where they dine, the evening has no ending imposed on it — no convoys, no curfews, no goodbyes at eleven. San Luigi's cypress avenue and farmhouse courts give the house a complete stage with the machinery hidden inside it.

What we produced here

The house has produced at San Luigi's register across Tuscany — full-weekend formats with the party in residence — and holds the borgo in its working portfolio of venues visited, costed and rehearsed.

Planning your wedding at Borgo San Luigi

Assign the farmhouses like a seating chart — families together, friends by affinity — and the weekend starts working before the first toast. Monteriggioni and Siena sit minutes away for the day between events.

The estate in brief

  • Monteriggioni, Tuscany — between Siena and the Chianti hills
  • Hamlet of stone farmhouses; houses the full guest list on site
  • Nearby by the house: Borgo Stomennano
