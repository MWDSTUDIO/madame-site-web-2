Borgo Stomennano: a Chianti village given over to one wedding

Borgo Stomennano is a private hamlet in the Chianti hills — houses around a square, gardens, olive groves — taken whole for one family at a time. The house produced a wedding weekend here that Style Me Pretty published in 2026.

Why Borgo Stomennano

Because a borgo answers the question every multi-day wedding asks — where does everyone live? — with the best possible answer: here, together. Stomennano sleeps the whole party inside the celebration itself; breakfast is a piazza, the ceremony is a garden, and nobody ever calls a car.

What we produced here

Claire & Jack's weekend — a Philadelphia couple, crystal chandeliers rigged over the courtyard, crimson blooms against five-hundred-year-old stone. Style Me Pretty published the wedding in June 2026.

Planning your wedding at Borgo Stomennano

Take it for no fewer than three nights and move every meal: the borgo's gift is variety inside intimacy. Chianti's roads reward early transfers and a production office that knows the local artisans by name — both are what the house is for.
