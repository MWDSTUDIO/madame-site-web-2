The Ritz Paris: producing a wedding on Place Vendôme

The Ritz is the most storied address in Paris, and it treats a wedding as the hotel has always treated its guests: flawlessly, and strictly on its own codes. Producing here means knowing them.

Why the Ritz Paris

Because some rooms carry a century of evenings in their walls. The Salon d'Été, the Grand Jardin in season, suites that have hosted everyone worth hosting — the Ritz gives a wedding an authority no décor can buy. Proust wrote here; Chanel lived here. A celebration at this address begins with history already seated.

What we produced here

The house has produced private celebrations at the Ritz and works its codes fluently — load-ins that never cross a guest's path, florals conditioned off-site, the hotel's own maîtres d'hôtel woven into our running order.

Planning your wedding at the Ritz Paris

Come with the guest list settled — the salons reward precision more than scale — and give the hotel's calendar the better part of a year. The Ritz does not bend its standards for anyone, which is exactly why it is worth commissioning a house that has met them before.

The address in brief

  • 15 Place Vendôme, Paris — reopened 2016 after a four-year restoration
  • Salons for dinners intimate to grand; the Grand Jardin in season
  • Nearby by the house: Hôtel d'Évreux, across the square
