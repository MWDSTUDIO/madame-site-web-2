The House

A house for the world's most considered celebrations

Madame Wedding Design is a wedding design and production house founded by Estelle Bogaert. Its specialty is full wedding planning service & production, worldwide, for celebrations from $100,000 to $1,000,000 and beyond. The house is named among the 15 Best Wedding Planners in Europe by The Wed, Top 5 in Paris and Top 10 in France by Wezoree, and its work has been featured in seven editions of Vogue, in Martha Stewart Weddings, and in Brides.

How a commission unfolds

A commission begins with a conversation and ends, on average, eighteen months later, when the last car leaves the estate. Between the two sit the phases we run for every family: strategy and budget architecture in the first month; the venue secured — often an estate that never reaches the market — by the third; creative direction locked as one coherent world by the sixth; artisans commissioned by name through the year; and a production calendar that choreographs arrivals from four continents to the minute. One house holds every thread. Our families are guests at their own celebration.

The standards of the house

We accept a limited number of weddings each year, by enquiry and referral, so that each receives the full attention of the house. We answer every enquiry personally, within 48 hours. We write weather plans nobody will know existed. We put non-disclosure agreements, closed venues and vetted teams at the base of every production, and we hand the press to our families on their terms — published only when, and how, they wish.

Estelle Bogaert, Founder & Creative Director

Estelle Bogaert founded the house on a conviction learned in production: that the most moving celebrations are the most rigorously prepared ones. She leads every commission herself — venue negotiation, artisan direction, the design of the tables and of the timeline alike — with a small, senior team around her.

Recognition

The house is named among the 15 Best Wedding Planners in Europe by The Wed and among the Top 5 planners in Paris and Top 10 in France by Wezoree. Its commissions have appeared in seven editions of Vogue, in Martha Stewart Weddings, Brides, Style Me Pretty, Carats & Cake and Over The Moon. The full record lives in the press room.
