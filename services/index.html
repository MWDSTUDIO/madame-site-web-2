Services

How we accompany you

The house serves in three ways — full wedding planning & production, creative direction, and destination & logistics. Every commission is led personally by Estelle Bogaert.

Full Wedding Planning & Production

The complete commission, and the way most families work with us. We hold everything: strategy and budget architecture; the search and negotiation for estates and venues, including houses that never reach the market; the commissioning and direction of artisans by name; guest logistics across borders — charters, transfers, accommodation of entire estates; and the on-the-ground production of every hour, with contingency plans nobody will know existed.

What this commission includes

Eighteen months of production on average, a senior team led by the founder, one budget held to the line, weekly counsel, and a single point of accountability from the first conversation to the last candle.

Who it is for

Families planning a celebration of consequence — often multi-day, often abroad, always private — who want one house answerable for all of it.

Creative Direction

The aesthetic language of the celebration, composed as one coherent world: concept, spatial design, tablescapes, florals, lighting, paper, wardrobe counsel. We direct the ateliers and artisans who execute it — and we hold the design against the production realities of the venue, so nothing drawn is undeliverable.

What this commission includes

A complete creative concept, design documentation for every space and table, artisan selection and direction, and on-site art direction through the celebration itself.

Who it is for

Families who have production in hand — often through their own offices — and want the eye and the ateliers of the house.

Destination & Logistics

For celebrations imagined elsewhere and made here. Some families arrive with their vision already in trusted hands — a planner of their own, a family office, a designer they have worked with for years. What they need is our ground: the estates we know before they reach the market, the local ateliers and artisans, the jurisdictions and contracts, and the quiet diplomacy a village requires when one family takes it over for a week.

What this commission includes

Destination counsel and venue negotiation, vendor contracting under local jurisdictions, complete guest movement — charters, transfers, room blocks across entire estates — and production management on the ground in Italy, France, Portugal and beyond, in seamless partnership with the team you trust.

Who it is for

Families — and the planners and family offices who serve them — bringing a celebration to Europe, who expect the distance to be invisible.

We do not offer smaller formats. The house takes fewer commissions and holds them entirely.
